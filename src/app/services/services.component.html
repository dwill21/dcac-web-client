Services

DeWillis College Admissions Consulting offers several packages to meet the needs of client families
Comprehensive Package Our best value to families. Allows us to make the most impact on planning and admissions success! Through a series of consultations we assess the student’s high school curriculum and extracurricular activities, determine the qualities important to that student in selecting a college, and define the attributes a school must possess in order for him/her to be successful. We match our findings with our extensive knowledge of colleges to create a “best-fit” list of schools. We also develop an ACT/SAT preparation and testing timeline, discuss summer enrichment, and prepare students for college visits. Our work continues with essay topic development and editing, financial aid and scholarship guidance, interview preparation as needed, and college application assistance. Finally, we evaluate the student’s college acceptance offers to help determine the best award. We keep students on track and working toward deadlines! This package is offered to students 11th-12th grades, customized according to the grade in which you begin. Our best advice is “Start Early!" Starting early alleviates some of the stress inherent in the college search, and most importantly, it gives you choices.
